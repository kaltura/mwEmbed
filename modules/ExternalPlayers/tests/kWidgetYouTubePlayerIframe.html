KDP YouTube Iframe wrapper

This page shows the youtube wrapper with the Iframe YouTube player behind with Kaltura UI, even if the client does have Flash player installed

To create a YouTube entry:

  1. Make sure you have MediaSpace permissions to add YouTube media.
  2. Within MediaSpace, click on "Add New" and choose "YouTube".
  3. Insert YouTube content ID and click the "Preview" button.
  4. Save your media as "Unlisted" or "Published".
  5. Within MediaSpace, preview your YouTube media and click the "Share" button.
  6. If you want to embed this entry outside of MediaSpace, use the entry ID shown at the end of the share link in your embed code.
