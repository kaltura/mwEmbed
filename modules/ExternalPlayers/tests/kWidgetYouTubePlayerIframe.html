KDP YouTube Iframe wrapper

This page shows the youtube wrapper with the Iframe YouTube player behind with Kaltura UI, even if the client does have Flash player installed

To create a YouTube entry:

  1. MediaSpace Admin > YouTube:
    • Enable "YouTube" module.
    • Add the player ID of the V2 Kaltura player you would like to use as the wrapper for the YouTube content (ensure the player has the desired functionality, such as a share button).
  2. Find a public YouTube video and get the video ID.
  3. MediaSpace > "Add New" > "YouTube" (from the menu on the left hand side)
  4. Insert YouTube content ID and click the "Preview" button.
  5. Save your media as "Unlisted" or "Published".
  6. Within MediaSpace, preview your YouTube media and click the "Share" button.
  7. To embed this entry outside of MediaSpace, find the entry ID from the Share link and paste this into another embed code you have from another entry (there is no "Share" tab in MediaSpace from which to grab and embed code as with other "non-YouTube" entries.
